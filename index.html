Features

Hi to all PawSomer friends in the world!
Pawsome
is a free pet social network app designed to connect pets and pet owners around the world! We are a pet friend focused community and strive to give pet owners endless opportunities to socialize their pets and encourage healthy lifestyles.
Some of the PawSome features of our pet social media app:


Home Page: On this page, you can pawlike and pawcomment on the latest posts of the people you follow.


Discover Page: Use this section of the app the discover nearby paw-friends via their location and filter through endless playdate options by breed, age and more.


Notifications: You can access your messages, pawlikes and pawlover notifications from the notifications section.

Pet Profile: Personalize your experience by creating a social profile for your pet, from which you can connect with other friends and create your very own little packs. Also, you can access your pawlovers and pawlovings easily.


Chat Feature: Connect and chat with paw-friends near and far with the PawSome
chat feature! Chat with just one paw-friend or form a group chat to keep in touch with everyone at once.
